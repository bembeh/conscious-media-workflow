2. Title & Font Hierarchy

  • Maximum 3-5 Words: Keep headline statements extremely brief for mobile readers.
  • Headline Font: Use thick, tall fonts like Bebas Neue or Syne.
  • Editorial Accents: Pair with premium serifs like Cinzel for high-end credibility.
  • Dynamic Tilt: A -3deg title tilt breaks grid monotony.

3. Artist Cutout & Face Sizing

  • Rule of Thirds: Position faces on left or right vertical lines, aligning eyes near the upper third grid.
  • Face Sizing: Let the face occupy at least 30-40% of the vertical space to maximize emotional connection.
  • Backlight Glows: Add gold or red glows to isolate the portrait from dark backgrounds.

⚠️ Timestamp Danger Zone

Always keep critical text and face profiles out of the bottom right corner (the YouTube duration tag covers this area).

  2. Create a project, enable the Google Drive API and Google Sheets API.
  3. Configure the OAuth consent screen (add your email as test user).
  4. Create an OAuth Client ID (Web Application type).
  5. Add http://localhost:3005 to Authorized JavaScript Origins.
  6. Paste the Client ID below and save.
